## What is GAMUDA AI Academy is even about?

Gamuda AI Academy is a **3-month intensive bootcamp** that prepares you to pivot into full stack AI development. There, I learned all the tools needed for AI development, which include things like:
1. **Frontend** development with React
2. **Backend** development with FastAPI
3. **Database** management with PostgreSQL
4. **Cloud** deployment with Google Cloud Platform
5. AI integration using **Gemini API**

And so many details about how to integrate AI functionality into software development. We also have the opportunity to understand how AI works under the hood, and how to clearly understand what AI can do to solve real world problems. There are also trainers there to guide your path in learning AI development, and they are very helpful in answering all the questions that you have in mind about AI development.

But, it does not stop there 🤯, you also get to polish your soft skills as well! There will be mentors that come once a week to share on how to prepare for job interviews, ace in your public speaking, how to write a good resume, how to build your **own startup company!**, and many more.

## What do you expect to gain from this academy?

Since this is a IT bootcamp, one of the biggest benefit that you will gain is a fully AI powered capstone project that you can easily showcase whether on your LinkedIn profile, Github repository, or even your resume. This project will be a great conversation starter during your job interviews, and it will also show that you have the skills to build AI powered applications.

Other than that, you will also get the chance to meet industry experts from Gamuda itself during networking sessions to ask more about Gamuda's culture, what to improve and what to expect from Gamuda. Quite interesting for fresh graduates to get to know more about the company that they are applying for, and it is also a great opportunity to ask questions that you might have in mind about the company.

## My thoughts on this academy

Overall, I feel like this is a great opportunity for people to get their hands dirty with AI development, and people to pivot into tech industry. It was intense yes, but was a great ride for you to experience how develop tech products with AI integration, and how to solve real world problems with AI. The trainers and mentors are super helpful, learning environment was top-notch and the community was so supportive to help you grow as an AI developer. For me, I rate this bootcamp a ⭐ 10/10!
