## What is Gamuda AI Academy All About?

Gamuda AI Academy is a **3-month intensive bootcamp** designed to prepare participants for a career in full-stack AI development. During the program, I learned essential tools and technologies, including:

1. **Frontend** development with React
2. **Backend** development with FastAPI
3. **Database** management using PostgreSQL
4. **Cloud** deployment on Google Cloud Platform
5. AI integration with the **Gemini API**

The program dives deep into integrating AI functionality into software development. It also provides insights into how AI works under the hood and its potential to solve real-world problems. The trainers were incredibly supportive, always ready to answer questions and guide us through the learning process.

But that's not all 🤯. The academy also focuses on enhancing soft skills! Weekly mentoring sessions covered topics like preparing for job interviews, excelling in public speaking, writing a standout resume, and even building your **own startup company**.

## What Can You Gain from This Academy?

As an IT bootcamp, the biggest takeaway is the **AI-powered capstone project** you'll complete. This project is perfect for showcasing your skills on LinkedIn, GitHub, or your resume. It serves as a great conversation starter during job interviews and demonstrates your ability to build AI-powered applications.

Additionally, the academy offers networking opportunities with industry experts from Gamuda. These sessions are invaluable for fresh graduates, providing insights into Gamuda's culture, tips for improvement, and what to expect when applying to the company.

## My Thoughts on This Academy

Overall, I believe this academy is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to dive into AI development or transition into the tech industry. While the program is intense, it offers invaluable experience in developing tech products with AI integration and solving real-world problems. The trainers and mentors are incredibly supportive, the learning environment is top-notch, and the community is highly encouraging. For me, this bootcamp deserves a ⭐ 10/10!
